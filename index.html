Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nusa Meals.

Is this a meal subscription? +

Yes! Nusa Meals is a weekly/monthly meal subscription. You choose your plan, and we deliver fresh meals to your door on your preferred schedule. No contracts — pause or cancel anytime.

Is it suitable for foreigners living in Bali? +

Absolutely! Our meals are designed for both expats and locals. We use familiar Indonesian flavors with strict hygiene standards, so you get authentic taste without worrying about food safety.

Can I see the ingredients before ordering? +

Yes! Every meal on our menu includes a full ingredient list. We're transparent about sourcing — all ingredients come from verified local farms and suppliers.

Is nutrition information included? +

Every meal comes with detailed macro breakdowns (calories, protein, carbs, fat). Our nutritionist designs each meal to be balanced and aligned with common dietary goals.

Where do you deliver in Bali? +

We deliver across Bali including Canggu, Seminyak, Ubud, Denpasar, Sanur, Kuta, and surrounding areas. Delivery is included free with all plans.
